The Basic RiderCourse is best place to start once you've made the decision to learn how to ride. Covers the basics of operating a motorcycle and safety-oriented mental strategies. Motorcycles and helmets are provided for your use. Successful completion of this course (consisting of a three-hour eCourse, five hours of formal classroom activities, and ten hours of riding instruction conducted over two days, plus knowledge and skill tests) waive the license test in Colorado. The course may also earn you an insurance discount.
